Climate

Warm & dry climates

For joint pain, respiratory conditions, or simply a preference for sun without humidity. Destinations where the weather is genuinely therapeutic, not just pleasant.

Mobility

Flat & walkable cities

Destinations where daily errands are manageable on foot — flat terrain, real sidewalks, accessible transit, and a layout that doesn't require a car to live well.

Budget

Fixed income friendly

Where a modest monthly income covers rent, healthcare, food, and a real life — with enough cushion that one bad month doesn't undo everything.

Healthcare

Progressive illness & chronic conditions

Destinations with reliable specialist access, affordable private care, and public systems that cover residents with pre-existing conditions.

Safety

Solo women

Where women moving alone report feeling genuinely free — not just statistically safe. Community, independence, and daily ease all factor in.

Pets

Pet-friendly destinations

Countries with straightforward pet import rules, no lengthy quarantine, good veterinary access, and a culture that's genuinely comfortable with animals.

Healthcare

Healthcare access

Destinations with strong public or private healthcare systems, English-speaking doctors, and costs that don't require international insurance to manage.

Proximity

Closest to the US

For those who want to stay connected — short flights home, familiar time zones, same-day returns possible in an emergency.

Safety

Safest countries

Ranked by the Global Peace Index and filtered for expat livability — places where safety is structural, not just a good neighborhood pick.

Housing

Accessible housing

Destinations where finding a single-level, elevator-accessible, or step-free rental is realistic — not a lucky exception.

Finance

Dollar economies

Countries that use the U.S. dollar — no exchange rate risk, no currency math. Your fixed income is what it is, every month.

Current conditions

Destinations under stress

Places that were popular retirement destinations but currently have conditions worth knowing about before you commit — political, economic, or safety-related.

Hidden Gems

Underrated international destinations

Countries that consistently get overlooked in favor of the same six names — evaluated honestly against fixed income viability, healthcare access, active expat communities, and solo livability.
