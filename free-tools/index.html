Free Resources

Two tools. One clear path.

Most people research retirement abroad by collecting information until they're overwhelmed. These two tools give the process a structure — one to figure out where, one to figure out how to get there.

Step 1 — Destination Determination Workbook

Work through your must-haves before you fall in love with a country. Climate, healthcare access, mobility needs, pets, budget, and solo safety — mapped against what actually matters to you. Fillable PDF or print.

Download free Workbook

Step 2 — Moving Abroad Action Planner

Once you've chosen your destination, this planner walks you through the full process — residency and legal, money and banking, healthcare and insurance, housing, pets, logistics, safety net, and the first 90 days after you land. Start early on the slow things: residency paperwork and pet entry requirements can take many months. Fillable PDF or print.
