How this works

Two phases. Two very different questions.

Most people try to answer both at once and end up going in circles. The research gets cleaner when you separate them.

Phase 1

Where should I go?

Before you research countries, get clear on what you actually need from one. Climate, healthcare, budget, mobility, pets, safety, proximity to family. The destination that fits your life is not the one someone else ranked #1.
