# Closest to the US

Mexico (1–5 hours), Belize (2–3 hours), Costa Rica (3–6 hours), and Panama (3–5 hours) are the closest options. Costa Rica has the strongest safety record and most established expat infrastructure. Panama combines proximity with a dollar economy and strong healthcare. Belize offers English as the official language. Mexico's proximity is unmatched but safety varies significantly by region.
