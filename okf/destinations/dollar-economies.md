# Dollar Economies

Panama and Ecuador are the two most popular retirement destinations that use the US dollar as their official currency — no exchange rate risk, no currency math, your fixed income is exactly what it is every month. El Salvador also uses the dollar. For fixed-income retirees, a dollar economy removes one of the biggest financial variables from the equation.
