# Flat & Walkable Cities

Valencia Spain tops the list — 200km of pedestrian infrastructure, genuinely flat. Medellín's El Poblado and Laureles are flat with metro access. Cuenca Ecuador's UNESCO historic center is walkable and flat (high altitude caveat). Mérida Mexico is flat. Montevideo is flat. Avoid: Lisbon, Porto, Dubrovnik, San Miguel de Allende — all hilly with cobblestones.
