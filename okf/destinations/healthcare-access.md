# Healthcare Access

Malta and Belize have English as an official language — no language barrier to healthcare. Malaysia's private hospitals operate largely in English. Panama City, Medellín, and major Costa Rican hospitals have English-speaking staff. Portugal and Spain have English-speaking doctors findable in expat zones. France ranks #1 for expat healthcare with 100% coverage for serious conditions.
