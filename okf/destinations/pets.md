# Pet-Friendly Destinations

Costa Rica and Panama have straightforward entry requirements and no quarantine. Most of continental Europe follows the EU pet passport framework. Ecuador is relatively easy. Malaysia requires permits but no quarantine. Thailand requires quarantine. The most pet-friendly destinations combine easy import rules, good veterinary access, and a culture genuinely comfortable with animals.
