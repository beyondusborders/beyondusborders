# Progressive Illness & Chronic Conditions

France's healthcare system covers serious chronic conditions at 100% with no exclusions or co-pays — the strongest public coverage for progressive illness. Portugal and Spain offer good specialist access with no pre-existing exclusions in their public systems. Costa Rica's Caja covers residents. Colombia has world-class private hospitals at low cost. The right answer depends on your specific condition and likely trajectory.
