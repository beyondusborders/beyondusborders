# Solo Women Destinations

Portugal consistently ranks strongest — women report walking alone at midnight feeling genuinely unbothered. Uruguay is safest in South America. Costa Rica leads Central America. Each destination on this list has an established solo female expat community, manageable visa requirements, and a track record of women reporting genuine daily independence — not just statistical safety.
