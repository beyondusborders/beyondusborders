# Underrated International Retirement Destinations

Destinations evaluated against five criteria: fixed income viability, healthcare access, active expat community with solo travelers represented, solo livability, and terrain and mobility considerations.

## Full Recommendations
- **Tbilisi, Georgia** — 365-day visa-free for US citizens, zero pension tax, real expat community, good private healthcare. Political situation warrants monitoring.
- **Albania (Tirana & Sarandë)** — Easiest European entry for Americans, 12-month visa-free, zero pension tax, lowest cost in Europe. Developing-country infrastructure.
- **Montevideo, Uruguay** — Safest in South America, unique mutualista healthcare ($100–200/month comprehensive), 11-year foreign income tax holiday. Real winters, smaller American community.

## Worth Watching
- **Burgas, Bulgaria** — Ultra-low cost, EU residency, Schengen access. Expat community primarily European not American.
- **Kotor & Tivat, Montenegro** — Beautiful, safe, affordable. Healthcare limited for complex conditions.

## Honest Mentions
- **Chitré, Panama** — Good amenities, dollar economy, but thin expat community. Spanish needed.
- **Pécs, Hungary** — Beautiful and affordable but no pension-based visa for US citizens as of 2026.
