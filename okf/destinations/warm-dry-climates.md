# Warm & Dry Climates

Low humidity and consistent warmth are the key variables — not just heat. High humidity makes joint pain and respiratory conditions worse, not better. Top destinations: Medellín (eternal spring, low humidity), Boquete Panama, Cuenca Ecuador (cooler but dry), Portugal's Algarve (warm, dry, 300+ days sun), Valencia and Andalusia Spain. Avoid tropical coastal climates for therapeutic benefit.
