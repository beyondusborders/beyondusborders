# Beyond US Borders Knowledge Bundle

Beyond US Borders is a static research site organized around the questions that actually decide whether retiring abroad works — not rankings, not beach photos, not affiliate wish lists.

## Audience

American women 55+ on fixed or modest incomes, often managing health conditions, mobility considerations, pets, and solo relocation.

## Content Structure

* [Topics](topics/index.md) — research guides covering what to know before choosing a destination
* [Destinations](destinations/index.md) — guides organized by specific need, not country rankings
* [Quick Reference](quick-reference/index.md) — direct answers to common questions, each linking to the full guide

## About This Bundle

All content is general research and information only — not financial, legal, medical, tax, or immigration advice. Each concept links to its published page at beyondusborders.com.
