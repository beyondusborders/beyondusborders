# Beyond US Borders

Beyond US Borders is a static research site organized around the questions that actually decide whether retiring abroad works — not rankings, not beach photos, not affiliate wish lists.

## Audience

American women 55+ on fixed or modest incomes, often managing health conditions, mobility considerations, pets, and solo relocation.

## Content Structure

- [Topics](/okf/topics/index.md) — research guides covering what to know before choosing a destination
- [Destinations](/okf/destinations/index.md) — guides organized by specific need, not country rankings
- [Quick Reference](/okf/quick-reference/index.md) — direct answers to common questions, each linking to the full guide

## About This Bundle

This OKF bundle represents the knowledge structure of beyondusborders.com. Each concept maps to a published page. All content is general research and information only — not financial, legal, medical, tax, or immigration advice.
