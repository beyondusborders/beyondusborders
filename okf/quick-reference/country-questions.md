# Country Questions

**Is Panama safe for solo retirees?** In established expat neighborhoods — El Cangrejo, Boquete — yes, with normal urban awareness. Strong Pensionado visa infrastructure, dollar economy, good healthcare.

**Cost of retiring in Costa Rica on a fixed income?** Realistic single-person budget $1,800–2,500/month in the Central Valley. Pensionado visa requires $1,000/month minimum but most need more. Excellent value for safety, healthcare, climate, and community.

**Is Portugal cold in winter for joint pain?** The Algarve: mild winters, rarely below 50°F, 300+ days sun — genuinely therapeutic. Lisbon and Porto: cool, rainy, occasionally cold. Northern Portugal is not the answer for cold-sensitive conditions.

**Is Ecuador safe and affordable for a single person?** Cuenca has a strong established solo expat community, safe by Latin American standards, walkable historic center. Dollar economy removes currency risk. Cost of living genuinely low.

**Panama vs Costa Rica — which is better?** Panama: dollar economy, stronger healthcare discounts, shorter East Coast flight. Costa Rica: better public healthcare (Caja covers pre-existing), spring-like climate, stronger solo safety record.

**Portugal vs Spain for a fixed income?** Portugal's D7 (~€920/month) significantly lower than Spain's NLV (~€2,400/month). Portugal ranks higher for solo safety. Spain offers better walkability (Valencia) and chronic illness management.
