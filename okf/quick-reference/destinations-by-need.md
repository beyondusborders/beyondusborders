# Destinations by Need

**Closest and safest countries to retire near the US?** Mexico (1–5 hrs), Belize (2–3 hrs), Costa Rica (3–6 hrs), Panama (3–5 hrs). Costa Rica has strongest safety record. Panama combines proximity with dollar economy.

**Best retirement destinations for a fixed income?** Ecuador (Cuenca) and Colombia (Medellín) offer the most livable combination of low cost, good healthcare, and real infrastructure. Portugal's interior towns offer best European value.

**Best climate for joint pain or respiratory conditions?** Low humidity and consistent warmth are the key variables. Medellín (70–80°F, low humidity), Boquete Panama, Cuenca Ecuador (cooler but dry), Portugal's Algarve, Valencia and Andalusia Spain.

**Best countries for accessible rental housing?** Medellín, Panama City, and Cuenca have good stocks of accessible apartment rentals. Avoid historic old towns — buildings predate accessibility standards.

**Countries that use the dollar with low visa thresholds?** Panama (~$1,000/month Pensionado) and Ecuador (~$1,446/month Jubilado) — both use the US dollar and have accessible income thresholds for Social Security and SSDI recipients.
