# Safety & Independence

**Is it safe for a woman to retire abroad alone?** Yes — thousands of American women do it every year and many report feeling safer than in the US. The research that matters is neighborhood-level, not country-level statistics.

**Safest countries for solo retirees?** Portugal ranks 7th globally — women report walking alone at midnight feeling unbothered. Uruguay is safest in South America. Costa Rica leads Central America. Malta ranks very high globally.

**How to research neighborhood-level safety?** Start with expat Facebook groups for women in your target city. Ask specific questions: where do women walk alone at night, which neighborhoods to avoid, what daily life feels like. The Global Peace Index gives country-level data; neighborhood reality requires talking to people who live there now.

**Best country for a solo retiree on a fixed income?** Portugal (interior towns for budget), Costa Rica (Central Valley), Ecuador (Cuenca), Panama (El Cangrejo, Boquete). Each has an established solo expat community and a track record of genuine daily independence.
