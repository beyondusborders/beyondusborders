# Fixed Income Reality Check

The real question isn't "is it cheap?" It's whether your specific monthly income covers your specific real-life costs — including healthcare, medications, pet care, and emergencies — with enough cushion that one bad month doesn't undo everything. Expat cost estimates are often written by people who own property, have a partner, or arrived years ago when prices were lower.
