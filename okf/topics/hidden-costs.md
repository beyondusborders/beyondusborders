# The Costs Nobody Warns You About

The costs expats most consistently underestimate: the move itself, international health insurance before residency kicks in, return flights home, currency conversion fees, the gap between tourist and resident prices, and the time and legal fees involved in residency paperwork. Budget for the transition period separately from ongoing monthly costs.
