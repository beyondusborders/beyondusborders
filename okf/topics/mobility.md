# Mobility & Daily Infrastructure

Walkable means daily errands are manageable on foot. Accessible means infrastructure accommodates mobility aids, limited stamina, or balance issues. A city can be walkable but not accessible. Your specific mobility situation determines which metric matters more. Research the specific neighborhood, not just the city.
