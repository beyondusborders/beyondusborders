# Moving Abroad With Pets

Start at least 6 months out, longer if your destination requires a rabies titer test — the test requires a waiting period after vaccination, then lab processing time. Most countries require microchip, up-to-date rabies vaccination, health certificate from a USDA-accredited vet, and official USDA endorsement. Thailand requires 30-day quarantine. Australia and New Zealand have strict quarantine requirements.
