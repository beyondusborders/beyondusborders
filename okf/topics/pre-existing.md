# Pre-Existing Conditions & International Healthcare

A pre-existing condition changes the research without disqualifying you. Some countries' public healthcare systems cover residents with no pre-existing exclusions — Costa Rica's Caja, Portugal's SNS, France's system. Private international insurers vary widely — some exclude pre-existing conditions entirely, others cover them with waiting periods.
