# How to Start Your Research

Don't start with countries. Start with yourself. Before researching destinations, get clear on your non-negotiables: exact monthly income, healthcare requirements, mobility needs, whether pets are coming, and how far you're willing to be from the US. The right order saves months of misdirected research.
