# Solo Female Safety Abroad

Thousands of American women retire abroad alone every year and many report feeling safer than in the US. The research that matters is neighborhood-level: foot traffic, lighting, how women move through public space, and what the local solo female expat community reports from daily experience. Statistics are a starting point, not an answer.
