# SSDI & Social Security Abroad

For most recipients, SSDI and Social Security retirement benefits continue if you live abroad — but not automatically for every country or benefit type. SSI stops when you leave the US. Retirement and SSDI generally continue. Country-specific restrictions apply to about 25 nations. Verify your specific benefit type and destination before assuming continuity.
