# Shipping Your Car vs Starting Over

Shipping costs $1,000–$5,000+, many countries have age restrictions that block older vehicles entirely, and import taxes can add significantly to the cost. Most expat destinations have affordable local transportation. The exceptions: newer vehicle, rural living, mobility equipment mounted in the car, or genuinely limited local transportation.
